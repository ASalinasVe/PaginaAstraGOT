The War of the Houses of Westeros

The Iron Throne, forged from the swords of fallen enemies, is the most coveted prize in Westeros. The houses Stark, Lannister, Targaryen, and Baratheon have fought, schemed, and sacrificed everything for this symbol of absolute power. In Westeros, to reign means to risk everything… and no king or queen remains safe for long.
